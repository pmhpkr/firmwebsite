About

Hari Prasad M.S. is an advocate based in Bengaluru with over 15 years of experience in dispute resolution, corporate advisory, and regulatory matters.

His practice encompasses commercial litigation, arbitration, constitutional and appellate matters, indirect taxation, company law, foreign exchange regulations, insolvency, intellectual property, real estate, and consumer disputes. He regularly appears before the High Court of Karnataka, tribunals, regulatory authorities, and other judicial and quasi-judicial forums.

Hari is also a qualified Company Secretary and advises clients on corporate structuring, regulatory compliance, foreign direct investment, contract management, and transaction advisory matters.

Professional Background

His professional experience includes leadership roles in dispute resolution practices and positions with law firms and professional services organisations, including Shivadass Law Chambers, Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan Attorneys, Ernst & Young, Crestlaw Partners, and B.C. Seetharama Rao & Associates.

Qualifications

  • B.A., LL.B. from Bangalore University
  • Associate Member, Institute of Company Secretaries of India
  • Post Graduate Diploma in Intellectual Property Rights
  • Master's in Business Law, National Law School of India University, Bengaluru
