Disclaimer: For appeals involving a tax / ITC demand, Section 107(6) requires pre-deposit of 10% of the remaining disputed tax or ITC. Section 112(8) requires a further 10%, bringing the total to 20%. For penalty-only orders: where the appeal is filed before 1 October 2025, no statutory pre-deposit is required on the penalty amount as Section 107(6) refers only to "tax in dispute" (affirmed by several High Courts including the Karnataka High Court). Where the appeal is filed on or after 1 October 2025, the Finance Act, 2025 amendment requires pre-deposit of 10% of the disputed penalty amount. Cases under Sections 73, 74, 122, 125, 129 or 130 may involve transitional considerations. Do not rely solely on this calculation. Always verify against the applicable provisions and the relevant order before making payment.
