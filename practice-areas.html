Practice Areas

The practice focuses on advisory, drafting, dispute resolution, and representation across the following areas:

Indirect Tax and GST

Advisory, litigation, and dispute resolution relating to Goods and Services Tax (GST), Service Tax, Central Excise, Customs, and allied indirect tax laws. Services include notices and investigations, audits, appellate proceedings, refund matters, classification and valuation disputes, and writ proceedings before constitutional courts.

Commercial Litigation

Representation and advisory services in commercial disputes, including contractual claims, shareholder disputes, recovery proceedings, injunctions, and proceedings before Commercial Courts and Commercial Appellate Divisions.

Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution

Drafting and review of arbitration agreements, representation in domestic arbitration proceedings, applications under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, and enforcement or challenge of arbitral awards.

Constitutional and Writ Matters

Advisory and representation in matters involving judicial review, administrative actions, statutory interpretation, and enforcement of legal and constitutional rights before High Courts.

Consumer Law

Representation of businesses and individuals in consumer disputes involving goods and services, product liability claims, warranty disputes, and proceedings before consumer commissions.

Contract Drafting and Advisory

Drafting, review, negotiation, and advisory services relating to commercial agreements, master service agreements, statements of work, technology contracts, licensing arrangements, confidentiality agreements, and vendor agreements.

Real Estate and Property Matters

Advisory and dispute resolution relating to property transactions, title verification, lease agreements, development agreements, apartment association matters, and regulatory compliance.

Corporate and Regulatory Advisory

Legal support on regulatory compliance, corporate governance, risk assessment, and business operations across various sectors.

Intellectual Property

Advisory and assistance relating to trademarks, licensing arrangements, brand protection, and intellectual property-related agreements.
