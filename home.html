About This Practice

The practice focuses on advisory, drafting, dispute resolution, and representation across indirect taxation (GST, Customs, Excise), commercial litigation, arbitration, constitutional matters, consumer law, contract advisory, real estate, corporate compliance, and intellectual property.

Key Areas of Practice

Indirect Tax & GST

Advisory, litigation, and dispute resolution relating to GST, Service Tax, Customs, and allied indirect tax laws. Services include notices, audits, appellate proceedings, and refund matters.

Commercial Litigation

Representation and advisory in commercial disputes, contractual claims, shareholder disputes, and proceedings before Commercial Courts and Commercial Appellate Divisions.

Arbitration

Drafting and review of arbitration agreements, representation in domestic arbitration proceedings, and enforcement or challenge of arbitral awards.

Corporate Advisory

Legal support on regulatory compliance, corporate governance, risk assessment, and business operations across various sectors.
